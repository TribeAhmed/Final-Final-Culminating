Tackling

Tackling in soccer is the defensive skill of legally intercepting or dispossessing an opponent of the ball. It requires precise timing and technique to win possession without committing fouls. Successful tackling is crucial for defenders to break up attacks and regain control of the ball, contributing to the team's defensive strategy.

Defending

Defending in soccer involves preventing the opposing team from scoring by intercepting passes, blocking shots, and marking players. Defenders aim to disrupt the opponent's attack, maintain a solid backline, and protect their goal. Effective defending is a fundamental aspect of a team's overall strategy, ensuring resilience and minimizing scoring opportunities for the opposition.
